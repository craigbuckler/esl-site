<p class="center wb"><a href="#" data-email="" class="nodec"><svg><use xlink:href="#svg-email"></use></svg> <span>info {at} exeter-student-lets dot co dot uk</span></a></p>

Please provide:

* the address of the property
* your name
* your telephone number
* the names of the other students

All properties are let on a joint agreement to a group of students. Single rooms are not available individually.

To reserve a property after viewing, all students must:

1. Provide their details, their guarantor's details, and sign the **Initial Holding Deposit** agreement.

1. Pay a holding deposit.

1. Sign the full **Licence to Occupy** agreement.

1. Pay the remainder of the deposit.

The process takes up to ten working days depending on the speed of student and guarantor responses. The full deposit is protected by [MyDeposits](https://www.mydeposits.co.uk/) and [returned](https://help.exeter-student-lets.co.uk/moving-out/deposit/) when the contract ends.
