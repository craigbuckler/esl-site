Hello.

We are George and Esther. We provide quality accommodation services and have helped hundreds of Exeter students for more than ${ tacs.config.trading } years.

We specialise in affordable, quality, fully-furnished student [flats](__/flat/) and [houses](__/house/) in prime locations close to the main [Exeter University](https://www.exeter.ac.uk/) Streatham campus, [St Luke's College](https://www.exeter.ac.uk/our-campuses/st-lukes-campus/), and the [Royal Devon &amp; Exeter Hospital](https://www.royaldevon.nhs.uk/).

We promise:

* **No agency fees!**

* Deposit protection by [MyDeposits](https://www.mydeposits.co.uk/) with prompt [return](https://help.exeter-student-lets.co.uk/moving-out/deposit/).

* Light and spacious rooms decorated to a high standard.

* [Clean, attractive, and stylish furniture](https://help.exeter-student-lets.co.uk/moving-in/inventory/).

* [Modern fitted kitchens](https://help.exeter-student-lets.co.uk/moving-in/inventory/#kitchen).

* [Weekly cleaning service](https://help.exeter-student-lets.co.uk/care/cleaning/).

* Fully compliant with <abbr title="House in Multiple Occupation">HMO</abbr> regulations.

* Regular gas, electricity, water, appliance, and fire safety inspections.

* An easy-to-use [online welcome and help pack](https://help.exeter-student-lets.co.uk/).

* [Houses](__/house/) and [flats](__/flat/) available to groups of students. (Note we cannot provide rooms on an individual basis in shared properties.)

Our friendly, professional, and prompt accommodation service provides a [rapid response to all queries](https://help.exeter-student-lets.co.uk/contacts/non-emergencies/). We maintain regular contact with our occupants and rectify problems quickly.
