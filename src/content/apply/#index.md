To arrange your viewing, please contact:

[07740 309 077](tel:+44-7740-309077)

<a href="#" data-email="">info {at} exeter-student-lets dot co dot uk</a>

Please include:

* the address of the property
* your name
* your telephone number
* the names of the other students

All properties are let on a joint agreement to a group of students. Single rooms are not available individually.

To reserve a property, all students must:

1. Provide their details, their guarantor's details, and sign the **Initial Holding Deposit** agreement.

1. Pay a holding deposit.

1. Sign the full **Licence to Occupy** agreement.

1. Pay the remainder of the deposit.

Deposits are protected by [MyDeposits](https://www.mydeposits.co.uk/).
